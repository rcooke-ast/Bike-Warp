ABOUT THE FONT

'STOWE OPEN FACE' is a high quality font containing full hinting for 
correct rendering at small point sizes and a total of 806 kerning pairs for 
correct character spacing. It is supplied 'as is' and no warranty is implied 
or given by the author. 'STOWE OPEN FACE' is a display font and is not
really suited to body text. It is best used at 18 points and above but will
render down to 8 point without trouble. The font contains a full range of
upper case letters, small capitals, the figures 0 to 9, a full range of
punctuation, and some diacritics. It was hand traced in order to achieve a
high quality outline and compiled using Type Designer 3.1. This
font represents approximately 300 man-hours of work.

The latest version of this font is always available for download from the 
file list of the fonts conference on the Cix conferencing system. The author 
would appreciate notification of any problems experienced with this font 
together with any suggestions for improvements.
